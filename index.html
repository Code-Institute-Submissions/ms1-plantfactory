Benefits of office plants

Reduce Stress

Having time outdoors is paramount in supporting our mental health. Getting enough time outdoors can be a struggle with our work patterns particulary in the winter. A study has shown that having plants in a workking enviroment can reduce emotional and physical stress.

Boost buisness aesthetic

Enhance your buisness potential for both new and existing clients and make your buisness a place that people want to work. Having plants in an office or professional space can increase visual interest making your buisness stand out and be a more enjoyable place.

Increases Productivity and Creativity

A study found that employees who work have natural elements within their work space reported an increase in creativity by 15%, are 6% more productive and had a well being score 15% higher compared to employees with no natural elements.
