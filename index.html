Benefits of office plants

Reduce Stress

Having time outdoors is paramount in supporting our mental health. Getting enough time outdoors can be a struggle with our work patterns particulary in the winter. A study has shown that having plants in a workking enviroment can reduce emotional and physical stress.

Boost buisness aesthetic

Enhance your buisness potential for both new and existing clients and make your buisness a place that people want to work. Having plants in an office or professional space can increase visual interest making your buisness stand out and be a more enjoyable place.

Increases Productivity and Creativity

A study found that employees who work have natural elements within their work space reported an increase in creativity by 15%, are 6% more productive and had a well being score 15% higher compared to employees with no natural elements.

What we offer

Bespoke Plant Features

Want something individual and eye catching to suit your business needs? Then a Bespoke Plant Feature is for you.

You will be paired with one of our highly experienced interior landscapers who take care of the design, installation, and maintenance of the feature. We will always arrange an on-site review before making suggestions and giving a quote. Whether you already have a style in mind or are looking for inspiration our team will be at your service.

Potted Plants

Need to make your workspace flourish or make your meeting areas more inviting?

You will be put into contact with one of our creative assistants to work out the types of plants your space will need and the size to make your buisness a place to thrive. We will also visit the plants once every 2 months for the first year to ensure they are getting the best care! Plant Factorys favourites include; Snake Plants, Weeping Fig and Money Tree.
